Early Bird Gets The Worm At These Trails!

Now that your familiar with Missouri Birding culture it's time to head out there! Below is an interactive map displaying three popular trails each at Central, Southwest, Southeast, Northwest and Northeast locations! Below the map you can find out more details about each location including the address, the trail's website and its corresponding eBird checklist (so you can see what birdies you might be able to spot). Enjoy the sights and sounds out there — and don't forget to have fun! *Disclaimer: This site page is currently still under construction and has an image of a map as a placeholder — but it will soon have a map once I learn how to embed an interactive Google map of sorts!*

  1. Address: 330 Commerce Dr, Jefferson City, MO 65109
  2. Phone: (573) 526-5544
  3. Website: columbia.org
  4. e-Bird List: ebird-hotspot.org

Mark Twain National Forest — Pine Ridge Recreation Area

  1. Address: 1545 Meadowmere Dr, New Bloomfield, MO 65063
  2. Driving Directions: From New Bloomfield, head west on Route MM 1 mile; Turn north on Route J to Guthrie; Then turn west on Route Y for 2 miles. From Ashland, go east on Route Y, 7 miles.
