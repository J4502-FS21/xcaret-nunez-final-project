Resident Birds To Keep An Eye Out For

Missouri is the most mid-state you can get – and that’s a good thing! Missouri is a place where a variety of of regions come together and create an array of bird habitats. In fact, Missouri has about 438 bird species with 340 of them considered residential birds. Below are five of the most common, you might even be able to find them in you backyard!
